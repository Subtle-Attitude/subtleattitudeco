Three Paths to Creative Self-Discovery

Atlas is a three-category prompt system designed to guide users through introspection, imagination, and dreamwork. Each deck offers a distinct lens for exploring the self:

  • Your Atlas
    Prompts focused on the user's past, present, and future—designed to spark inner dialogue and emotional clarity.
  • Envision
    Prompts that place the user in their most ideal future, encouraging visualization and alignment with actionable steps toward that life.
  • Dreamcatcher
    Prompts crafted to enhance dream recall and increase metacognition, supporting lucid dreaming and subconscious exploration.
